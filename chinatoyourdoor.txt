IV. Service Page Logic & Content Structure
1. البحث عن منتج (Find a Product)
Form Fields:

الاسم الكامل (Name)

رقم الهاتف (Phone Number)

اسم المنتج (Product Name - Free text entry in AR or EN)

الكمية المطلوبة (Quantity - Optional)

تحميل صورة المنتج (Upload Product Image - File Upload Button)

Action:

Submit Button: On submission, the data is captured and formatted to be inserted into a cloud-based Excel/Sheet file (simulating the chinatoyourdoor inquires.xlsm structure).

Confirmation: A "Thank you" message appears, followed by a recommendation to use the "Chat with Us" button for immediate follow-up.

2. الشحن فقط (Shipping Only)
Input Fields:

طريقة الشحن (Shipping Method): Radio button/Toggle switch for "شحن جوي" (By Air) or "شحن بحري" (By Sea).

نوع المنتج (Product Type)

الوزن الإجمالي (كجم) (Total Weight in KG)

الحجم الإجمالي (متر مكعب) (Total Volume CBM)

Calculation Logic:

IF By Air: Uses Weight as the primary factor.


Cost (USD): Total Weight (KG) × (9 to 10 USD) 


Duration: ≈ 15 days.

IF By Sea: Uses CBM as the primary factor.


Cost (IQD): Total CBM × 250,000 IQD.


Duration: ≈ 40 days.

Output:

The system displays the calculated Shipping Cost (in IQD or USD, based on method) and the Estimated Duration.


Disclaimer Note: "ملاحظة: تُحسب تكلفة الشحن بدقة حسب نوع المنتجات وحجمها/وزنها." 

3. شراء فاتورة وشحن (Purchasing a Bill and Shipping)
Input Fields:

قيمة الفاتورة الإجمالية (Total Bill Amount in USD)

طريقة الشحن (Shipping Method): Toggle for Air or Sea.

الوزن الإجمالي (كجم) (Total Weight in KG)

الحجم الإجمالي (متر مكعب) (Total Volume CBM)

Calculation Logic (Two Components):

Service & Payment Commission:


Commission Rate: Use 3% of the invoice value (since this implies a single, large bill). 

Commission Value (USD): Total Bill Amount (USD) × 0.03


Conversion Rate: 1 USD = 1450 IQD.

Total Initial Cost (IQD): (Total Bill Amount (USD) × 1450) + (Commission Value (USD) × 1450)

Shipping Fee: (Uses the same logic as Shipping Only).

Air: Total Weight × (9 to 10 USD)

Sea: Total CBM × 250,000 IQD

Output:

Detailed Results Screen: Clearly shows the breakdown:

Original Bill Value (IQD).

Company Commission (3%) in IQD.

Shipping Fee (IQD).

Total Estimated Cost (IQD).

Note: "للمزيد من التفاصيل أرسل لنا رسالة."

Sharing Options: Buttons for "مشاركة عبر واتساب" (Share via WhatsApp) and "نسخ الرابط" (Copy Weblink).

4. شراء من متاجر صينية (Purchase from Chinese Stores)
Calculation Logic:

This uses the SAME calculation structure as Purchasing a Bill and Shipping, but the Commission Rate is fixed at 5% (to account for the added complexity of sourcing from multiple suppliers and using التخزين والتجميع ).

Commission Rate: 5% of the invoice value.

Output:

Same detailed results and sharing options as Purchasing a Bill and Shipping.

6. تتبع شحنتك (Track Your Shipment)
Input Fields:

طريقة الشحن (Shipping Method): Toggle for Air or Sea.

تاريخ الشحن (Shipping Date - English Calendar Date Picker)

Calculation Logic (using English Calendar):


IF By Air: ETA = Shipping date + 15 days.


IF By Sea: ETA = Shipping date + 50 days (Using a slight adjustment from the 40-day estimate for delivery complexity).

Output:

Visual Result: A 3D motion map animation shows a line/icon moving from China (e.g., Yiwu/Guangzhou) to Iraq (e.g., Baghdad/Erbil).

Text Result: The calculated Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) date is displayed prominently.
